What is this assignment actually asking you to do?

You work for a local chamber of commerce. A small business owner has come to you and asked you to help them get started on social media. Your job is to:

1Find a real small business and understand what they want to achieve
2Make a plan for how they should use social media
3Get feedback on your plan from the business owner, then improve it
4Actually create the social media accounts and post content
5Collect data and analyse how well your posts performed
6Evaluate everything — what worked, what didn't, and what you'd do differently

Important

Academic Integrity

Read this carefully before you start.

✅

You ARE allowed to use AI for:

  • Generating or editing images for your social media posts
  • Helping draft captions — as long as you adapt it yourself
  • Checking grammar or improving your writing
  • Research and brainstorming ideas
❌

You are NOT allowed to:

  • Make up or fabricate analytics data, likes, comments or reach
  • Use AI to generate fake screenshots of posts or dashboards
  • Invent feedback or meeting notes from the business owner
  • Copy-paste AI-written evaluations as your own work
📊

About your data

Your analytics do not need to be impressive. Even 1 like is fine — use it. What matters is that your analysis is honest and thoughtful.

⚠️

Consequence of fabricating data

If you fabricate data, screenshots, or misrepresent your work:

  • Your assignment will be returned for resubmission
  • Your grade will be capped at a Pass — regardless of everything else
